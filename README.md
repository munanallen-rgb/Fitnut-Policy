# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to Fitnut app and the related backend services used to operate the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated when you use the app, and limited technical information required to operate the service.

### 1.1 Account and authentication information

Depending on how you sign in, we may collect:

- your email address;
- account authentication data;
- your Apple Sign In identifier or token data needed to authenticate your account;
- your username, avatar, user ID, or similar profile identifiers associated with your account.

### 1.2 Profile and fitness information

If you choose to use the fitness and personalization features, we may collect:

- age;
- sex or gender selection;
- height and weight;
- fitness level;
- training goal;
- available equipment;
- preferred target area;
- injury history or physical condition notes that you choose to enter.

### 1.3 Workout and training records

We may collect and store:

- workout dates;
- exercise names and custom exercises;
- sets, reps, weight, RPE, notes, and training duration;
- training snapshots, restore records, and related history needed to provide sync and rollback features.

### 1.4 AI interaction data

If you use the AI coach features, we may collect and process:

- the messages and questions you submit;
- relevant recent conversation context;
- training summaries and profile context assembled by our systems to generate a response.

Some AI session history may also be stored locally on your device so that you can view prior conversations in the app.

### 1.5 Feedback and support information

If you submit feedback or contact us, we may collect:

- your message content;
- your account identifier, if applicable;
- any other information you choose to include in your support request.

### 1.6 Subscription and purchase information

If you purchase paid features through Apple In-App Purchase, we may collect or receive:

- product identifiers;
- transaction identifiers and original transaction identifiers;
- purchase status, restore status, subscription status, and expiration date;
- internal entitlement or quota information used to manage paid features in the app.

We do not directly store your full payment card number or Apple billing credentials.

### 1.7 Technical and usage information

We may process limited technical information such as:

- app language and locale;
- device or platform type;
- authentication state and access tokens;
- server-side request logs needed for security, reliability, and debugging;
- local app cache data such as login state, sync markers, and AI session cache.

The current codebase does not indicate that we intentionally collect contacts, photo library content, camera captures, or precise location data as part of the core app workflow.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- create and manage your account;
- authenticate you and keep you signed in;
- save, sync, display, and restore your workout records and custom exercises;
- personalize your training experience;
- generate AI coach responses and training analysis;
- process subscriptions, restores, entitlement checks, and internal usage balances;
- respond to feedback and provide user support;
- prevent abuse, fraud, misuse, and security incidents;
- maintain, debug, improve, and monitor the service;
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only where necessary to operate the service or comply with law.

### 3.1 Infrastructure and backend providers

We use `Supabase` for authentication, database services, server functions, and related backend infrastructure. Your account data, workout data, support data, and service-related records may be processed through that infrastructure.

### 3.2 AI service providers

When you use the AI coach, relevant input and context are sent through our backend and then to third-party AI providers. Based on the current implementation, AI requests are routed through our Supabase Edge Functions to `SiliconFlow` and the model providers available through that service.

### 3.3 Apple services

If you use Apple Sign In or Apple In-App Purchase, Apple will process the information necessary to authenticate you, manage your subscription, and process billing, refunds, or purchase restoration under Apple's own terms and policies.

### 3.4 Legal disclosures

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request, or if reasonably necessary to protect our rights, users, or the security of the service.

### 3.5 Business transfers

If the app or its assets are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar transaction, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

## 4. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than the country in which you live. Those countries may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

Where required by applicable law, we will take reasonable steps to use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction that requires a legal basis for processing, we generally rely on one or more of the following:

- performance of a contract with you;
- our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the app;
- your consent, where required;
- compliance with legal obligations.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to provide the service, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

In general:

- account, profile, and workout data are retained while your account remains active;
- payment and audit-related records may be retained for longer if needed for accounting, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or legal compliance;
- support records may be retained for a reasonable support and recordkeeping period;
- locally cached data may remain on your device until you delete the app, clear app data, or sign out.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, export of, or restriction of the processing of your personal information.

You may also:

- update certain profile and workout information inside the app;
- stop using optional features, including AI features;
- request account deletion or data deletion by contacting us at `progresoapp@163.com`.

For security reasons, we may ask you to verify your identity before acting on a request.

Please note that some information may need to be retained for legal, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes even after a deletion request.

If you are in the EEA or UK, you may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including access controls, authentication mechanisms, and backend permission controls appropriate to the nature of the service.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 16, or under the minimum age required by the laws of your jurisdiction to provide valid consent.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child without the required consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may notify you through the app, on a website, or by other reasonable means. The updated version will become effective on the date shown at the top of the policy.
